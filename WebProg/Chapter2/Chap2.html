PARASITE (2019 FILM)

South Korean dark comedy thriller film directed by Bong Joon-ho

Watch Now

Plot Overview

"Parasite" is a captivating tale that delves deep into the intricate layers of social class dynamics, presenting a thought-provoking narrative that oscillates between comedy and tragedy. The film follows the Kim family, struggling to make ends meet in the cramped confines of their basement apartment. Their fortunes take a turn when their son, Ki-woo, lands a tutoring job with the affluent Park family, setting off a chain of deceitful machinations. As the Kims infiltrate the lives of the Parks under false pretenses, they skillfully manipulate their way into employment within the household. With each member posing as unrelated individuals, they revel in newfound comforts and opportunities, blurring the lines between reality and illusion. However, their carefully constructed facade begins to unravel when unexpected events expose their web of lies.

Director Bong Joon-ho masterfully crafts a narrative that escalates with tension and suspense, exploring themes of inequality, greed, and the consequences of societal disparity. The juxtaposition of the luxurious Park residence and the squalid living conditions of the Kims serves as a stark reminder of the vast chasm between the haves and have-nots. As the plot unfolds, "Parasite" evolves into a gripping and unpredictable commentary on the human condition, culminating in a shocking and unforgettable climax that challenges perceptions and leaves a lasting impact on viewers.
